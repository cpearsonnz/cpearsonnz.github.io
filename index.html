Executive Bio

Operational leader.
Commercial builder.

Chief Executive Officer and General Manager with extensive experience leading multi-region, service-based businesses across the property, infrastructure, construction and maintenance sectors.

Former CEO of Access | Partners in Property, operating across Auckland, Tauranga and Hamilton, delivering integrated property services including construction, project management, maintenance and specialist compliance solutions.

Trade-qualified plumber and drainlayer who progressed from founding and running a civil contracting business to senior executive leadership. Holds a Bachelor of Business and completed the Advanced Management Program at Melbourne Business School.

Known for decisive leadership, strong safety and customer focus, and the ability to translate strategy into action. As CEO, delivered 30% revenue growth over three years, supported by three successful acquisitions. Gallup Achiever profile.
